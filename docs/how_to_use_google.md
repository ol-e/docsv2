# How to Use Google

## Getting Started

1. Open a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.)
2. Go to [google.com](https://www.google.com)
3. Click the search bar in the center of the page

---

## Basic Search

Type any word, phrase, or question into the search bar and press **Enter** or click the **Google Search** button.

**Examples:**
- `weather Oslo`
- `how to boil an egg`
- `best restaurants near me`

---

## Search Tips & Tricks

### Phrase Search
Wrap words in quotes to search for an exact phrase.
```
"climate change solutions"
```

### Exclude Words
Use a minus sign to exclude a word from results.
```
jaguar -car
```

### Search a Specific Site
```
site:wikipedia.org Albert Einstein
```

### Find Similar Sites
```
related:nytimes.com
```

### Search for a File Type
```
filetype:pdf annual report 2024
```

### Use OR
```
marathon OR triathlon training plan
```

### Wildcard Search
Use `*` as a placeholder for unknown words.
```
"the * of the century"
```

---

## Google Tools & Filters

After searching, use the tabs and filters at the top of results to narrow down:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|---------------|
| **All** | All results |
| **Images** | Photos and graphics |
| **News** | Recent news articles |
| **Videos** | Video content |
| **Maps** | Location-based results |
| **Shopping** | Products for sale |

Use **Tools** (under the search bar) to filter by date, region, or exact match.

---

## Quick Answers (Without Clicking)

Google can answer many things directly in the search results:

- **Calculator:** `25 * 4 + 10`
- **Unit conversion:** `10 miles in km`
- **Currency:** `100 USD in EUR`
- **Time:** `what time is it in Tokyo`
- **Weather:** `weather this week`
- **Dictionary:** `define ephemeral`
- **Timer:** `set a 5 minute timer`

---

## Google Search Settings

1. Click **Settings** (bottom right of homepage, or gear icon on results page)
2. Adjust preferences like:
   - Safe Search (filter explicit content)
   - Results per page
   - Language and region

---

## Staying Safe on Google

- Stick to results from reputable sources
- Look for **HTTPS** in URLs before clicking
- Avoid clicking ads if you're looking for organic results (ads are labeled at the top)
- Be cautious with results that ask for personal information

---

*Tip: The more specific your search, the better your results.*
